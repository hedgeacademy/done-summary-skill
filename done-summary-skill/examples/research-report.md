# Done Summary Example: Research Report

## Goal

Produce a concise research brief comparing several options for a business decision.

## Who Did What

- User provided the decision context and preferred output style.
- AI agent gathered current source material, compared options, and wrote a structured brief.

## Why This Approach

The decision depended on current facts and tradeoffs. A structured brief made assumptions, evidence, and recommendations easy to review.

## Final Result

A research brief was delivered with recommendation, comparison table, caveats, and next actions.

## Verification

- Sources were checked for recency.
- Key claims were tied to cited evidence.
- Unverified assumptions were labeled.

## Pitfalls

Some sources used different definitions for the same metric. The brief normalized the terminology before comparing options.

## Risks And Open Items

Market conditions may change. The brief should be refreshed before a final commitment.

## Next Step

Pick one option for deeper validation or ask for a second-pass fact check.

## Reusable Knowledge

Research summaries should preserve source dates, definitions, assumptions, and confidence boundaries.
